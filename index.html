📰 Spotlight: Peniel Toxla – Making the All Nations University Constitution Accessible to All! 📘🎓

Meet Peniel Toxla, a dedicated and visionary student of All Nations University, who is using her voice and platform to bring “The Constitution at Your Doorstep – Everyday Online.” 🚪📚

Through her daily LinkedIn updates, Peniel breaks down key sections of the All Nations University Constitution — helping fellow students understand their rights, responsibilities, and the values that guide student life and governance.

Her goal? To make sure every student is informed, empowered, and actively engaged in shaping a better university environment.

Follow her and be part of the change. When students know the rules, they can lead with confidence and purpose.
